## Companion System

### Overview

- Recruitable NPCs (mostly female as per design direction)
- Some are combat-focused, others are utility/support

### Constraints

- Limited party slots
- Encourages build diversity

### Companion Attributes

- Affection (relationship level)
    - Unlocks required via quest or some other mechanism
- Trust (combat synergy)
- Skills (active/passive)
- Tags (element, faction, personality)
- Backstory (lore and dialogue)
- Combat or non-combat roles
    - Combat: DPS, Tank, Support
        - Will have their own equipment and progression
        - Can do certain roles (1,2 or 3)
        - Equipment matters, unique to companions, not shared with player
        - Some companions may prefer certain roles
    - Non-combat: Crafting, Gathering or other passive effects

### Acquisition

- Story progression
- Events
- Rare encounters
- Optional gacha-like mechanics

## Affection and Reputation

### Companion Affection

Unlocks:

- Skills
- Dialogue
- Bonuses
- Star Levels (it's the bonus, incresingly harder to get to the next one, but the bonus is better), increased by trust

### Faction Reputation

Unlocks:

- Shops
- Quests
- Raids
- Crafting recipes

## Questing

### Types

- Story quests
- Companion quests
- World quests
- Repeatable tasks
