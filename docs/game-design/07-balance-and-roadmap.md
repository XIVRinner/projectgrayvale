# Balance and Roadmap

[Back to index](../game-design.md) | [Previous: Economy and Base Systems](./06-economy-and-base-systems.md)

## Balance Philosophy

### No Nerfs Approach

- Strong builds are allowed
- Meta dominance is acceptable

### Counter-Based Design

Instead of nerfing, introduce enemies and mechanics that counter dominant builds.

### Exception Rule

Nerf only if output is extremely beyond intended scale.

**Example:**

- Expected: 10k damage
- Actual: 200M damage

## Future Systems

- Kingdom building expansion
- Multiplayer raids
- Advanced AI companions
- Procedural events
- Seasonal content

## Risks and Considerations

### System Overload

High number of systems requires phased development.

### Companion Scope

Large cast means high content cost.

**Possible solution:**  
Use modular design plus archetypes.

### Balance Complexity

The no-nerf philosophy requires strong encounter design.
